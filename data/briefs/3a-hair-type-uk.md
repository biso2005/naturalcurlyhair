### Gap 2: No Transparent Testing — Only Assertions
Top results make routine and product recommendations with no methodology. Claims like "this gel defines curls" are unverified opinion. **Exploit by:** publishing a repeatable test framework (panel size, hair porosity range, conditions tested) so every recommendation has a traceable source. Results present 3A as a stable category. In practice, the same head of hair can shift between 2C and 3A depending on porosity, product load and season. No current result addresses this honestly. **Exploit by:** showing documented examples from your test panel where curl pattern shifted, and explaining the variables that caused it — making the identification section genuinely more useful than anything currently ranking.

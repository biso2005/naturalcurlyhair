## 4. Gaps in Current Top Results to Exploit

### Gap 1 — The float test is presented uncritically
Every result that covers testing includes the float test as valid. It is widely acknowledged in trichology that product residue, coil compaction, and air trapped in tight curl patterns make this test particularly unreliable on 4C hair. **Exploit:** Test it, show the failure, redirect to more reliable observation-based methods.

### Gap 2 — Low porosity 4C is either dismissed or ignored
Result #2 argues 4C hair is *not* low porosity as a blanket statement. No result provides evidence either way or acknowledges that porosity is a spectrum influenced by damage history and genetics, not curl type. **Exploit:** Present original observations; distinguish between 4C hair that *behaves* low porosity vs. the assumption it cannot be.

### Gap 3 — No result covers mixed porosity
Longer 4C hair frequently shows high-porosity ends and low-porosity roots due to age of the hair shaft. This is absent from all top results. **Exploit:** Cover it directly with a how-to-identify section, and explain why a single routine may be fighting itself.

## 4. Gaps in Current Top Results to Exploit

### Gap 1 — No UK-specific environmental context
Every result gives universal timelines. The UK's predominantly hard water (particularly London, Midlands, South East) leaves mineral deposits that coat the hair shaft and demonstrably slow locking. No competitor mentions this. **Exploit by:** testing loc progression with and without a water filter or clarifying rinse, and reporting the difference.

### Gap 2 — "Mature" is never defined precisely
Results say "18–36 months" but never explain what physical markers confirm maturity (no unravelling under tension, consistent diameter, closed cuticle feel). Readers cannot self-assess. **Exploit by:** producing a concrete, testable checklist — pull test, texture description, growth measurement — so readers know exactly when they've crossed the threshold.

### Gap 3 — Starting method is underweighted
Competitors mention starting methods briefly but do not show how freeform, comb coils, two-strand twists, or interlocking produce different maturation speeds. **Exploit by:** tracking at least two starting methods in parallel and reporting timeline differences with photographic evidence.

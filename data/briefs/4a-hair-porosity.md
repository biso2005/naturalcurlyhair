## 3. Products to Consider (Generic)

> **Note to writer: Do not name brands. Verify all ingredient claims against published formulation data or INCI lists before publishing.**

- **Low porosity:** lightweight liquid leave-ins, humectant-forward products (glycerin, aloe vera), clarifying shampoos (remove buildup preventing moisture entry)
- **High porosity:** heavier butters and oils for sealing, protein treatments (hydrolysed keratin, wheat protein) used on a schedule — not indefinitely
- **All porosity types:** pH-balanced conditioners (ideally 4.5–5.5 to encourage cuticle closure); writer must confirm pH with brand or independent testing
- **Tools:** hooded dryer or steamer for low porosity (heat aids penetration); microfibre towel or cotton tee for high porosity (reduces mechanical friction)

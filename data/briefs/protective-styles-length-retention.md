## 4. Gaps in Current Top Results to Exploit

### Gap 1: No Original Data
Every ranking result relies on anecdote, opinion, or recycled advice. None presents a tested comparison of styles against a measurable retention metric. **Publishing even a small documented panel test (8–12 participants, 8–12 weeks, before/after measurements) will be unique in this SERP.**

### Gap 2: Duration and Scalp Care Are Ignored Together
Results either cover style duration *or* scalp care, never both as a linked system. Readers leaving styles in too long while neglecting scalp health is a documented cause of net length *loss* — this is the core failure mode nobody addresses directly.

### Gap 3: UK Hair Context Is Absent
All high-ranking content is US-produced. UK tap water is predominantly hard water, which affects moisture retention under protective styles differently. UK climate (low winter humidity) also changes how quickly styles dry out. **A UK-specific testing note here is a genuine differentiator with no current competition.**

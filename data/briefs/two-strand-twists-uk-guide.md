## 4. Gaps in Current Top Results to Exploit

**Gap 1 — Zero UK-specific context**
Every ranking result is US-based. UK tap water is significantly harder in the South East and Midlands, which affects how humectants and creams perform. A section grounded in this, backed by UK water hardness data from a regional supplier or the Drinking Water Inspectorate, is an immediate differentiator no competitor has.

**Gap 2 — No real testing evidence**
Top results are instructional but unverified. No result shows before/after images from a structured test, compares drying methods with measured outcomes, or discloses what went wrong. A brief testing methodology box (hair type, water type, products used, conditions) gives this page credibility that listicle-style guides structurally cannot match.

**Gap 3 — Protective wear vs. twist-out conflation**
Results 3, 5, and 10 blend both goals without separating technique. Readers wanting a week-long protective style need larger sections and heavier product; those prepping a twist-out need smaller sections and a lighter hand. A clear decision point early in the article captures both search intents and reduces pogo-sticking.

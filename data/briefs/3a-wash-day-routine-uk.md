## 4. Gaps in Current Top Results to Exploit

**Gap 1 — No UK-specific hard water guidance**
All top results are either US-based or UK sites that ignore regional water hardness. Roughly 60% of England is in a hard or very hard water zone. No result explains how this changes product choice, build-up frequency, or chelating shampoo cadence for 3A hair specifically.

**Gap 2 — No porosity-split advice**
Every top result treats 3A as a single category. Fine, low-porosity 3A hair over-conditions easily and needs different product weights than coarse, high-porosity 3A. Splitting the routine by porosity within the 3A type fills a real reader need and targets additional long-tail keywords.

**Gap 3 — No original testing or evidence**
Top results (TikTok, YouTube, Curlsmith blog) present routines as personal preference or brand recommendations with no comparative testing. A page that documents controlled, repeatable tests — same steps, different products, same water hardness zone — owns a credibility position none of the current ten hold.

## 3. Products to Consider (Generic — No Brand Claims)

- **Fragrance-free baby wash** — scalp-safe pH, no sulphates; note that adult curl products are often too heavy for fine baby hair
- **Light leave-in conditioner** — emollient but not occlusive; test for white cast on darker skin tones
- **Wide-tooth detangling comb** — verify tooth spacing appropriate for infant curl diameter
- **Satin or bamboo sleep bonnet / pillowcase** — friction reduction during texture transition
- **Water-based curl cream** — for when curl pattern begins emerging post-6 months; test hold vs. flaking

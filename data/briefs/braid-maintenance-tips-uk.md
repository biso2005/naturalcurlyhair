**Gap 1 — No UK-specific climate or water advice**
Every top result is written for a generic (largely US) audience. Hard water affects up to 60% of UK postcodes. No result addresses limescale residue on extensions or the impact of damp British winters on drying time. This is a clear, ownable angle.

**Gap 2 — No original testing or data**
All top results rely on stylist quotes or general best practice. None show a panel, a timeframe, or a measured outcome. A 4-week documented test with real UK participants (different braid styles, different hair types) would immediately differentiate this piece.

**Gap 3 — Take-down and breakage prevention is ignored or minimal**
The majority of results stop at maintenance during wear. Breakage most commonly occurs at take-down. A detailed, tested take-down method with tool comparisons (wide-tooth comb vs fingers vs detangling brush, timed, with shed-hair counts) is a practical gap none of the top 10 fills properly.

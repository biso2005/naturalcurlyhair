Brighton has a smaller Afro hair salon offering than most cities on this guide, reflecting its demographics and size, but it's not a complete desert. Mobile stylists covering Brighton and East Sussex fill the gaps.

**Brighton water:** very hard. South East Water and Southern Water serve Brighton with some of the hardest water in England — typically above 300mg/L, comparable to London. Monthly chelating shampoo is non-negotiable for natural hair in Brighton. Hard water mineral buildup will affect product performance noticeably.

Leicester has a large and long-established Caribbean community, particularly in the Highfields, Belgrave, and Evington areas, and an Afro hair salon offering that reflects this. The city often gets overlooked in UK natural hair coverage in favour of Birmingham or London, but Leicester has a substantial range of specialist salons relative to its size.

**Leicester water:** hard to very hard. Severn Trent serves much of Leicester with hard water — typically 250–300mg/L. Regular chelating shampoo (monthly or bi-monthly) is important for natural hair in Leicester to prevent mineral buildup.

**Instagram.** Search "#LeicesterNaturalHair", "#LeicesterBraids", "#AfroHairLeicester". Leicester has an active natural hair community on Instagram; look also at "#EastMidlandsHair" for stylists covering the broader region.

**Google Maps.** "Afro hair salon Leicester" or "natural hair Leicester". Highfields returns the most results.

**Highfields.** The historically highest concentration of Afro hair businesses in Leicester, including salons and Black-owned beauty supply stores.

**Leicester water is hard. What does that mean for my natural hair?**

Hard water deposits calcium and magnesium on the hair shaft over time, causing buildup that makes hair feel rough and products less effective. Monthly chelating shampoo (not just clarifying shampoo — they're different) addresses this. → [Shower filter vs chelating shampoo UK](/products/shower-filter-vs-chelating-shampoo-uk/)

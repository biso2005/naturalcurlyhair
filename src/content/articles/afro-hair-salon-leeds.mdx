Leeds has a smaller Afro hair salon offering than London or Birmingham, but a real and growing natural hair stylist community, particularly in Chapeltown, Harehills, and the Hyde Park area. For specialist natural hair services — especially type 4 hair and locs — the approach is the same as in any UK city: Instagram and Facebook group recommendations are more reliable than directory searches.

**Leeds water:** variable. Yorkshire Water covers Leeds and the hardness varies by local source. Some Leeds postcodes receive moderately hard water; others softer. Check Yorkshire Water's postcode checker if mineral buildup is a concern for your hair. A chelating shampoo quarterly is sensible regardless.

## How to Find a Leeds Afro Hair Salon

**Instagram.** Search "#LeedsNaturalHair", "#LeedsBraids", "#AfroHairLeeds". Leeds has an active natural hair community on Instagram; look also at "#WestYorkshireHair" as some stylists cover the broader region including Bradford, Halifax, and Huddersfield.

**Facebook groups.** Leeds-based Black community and natural hair groups are active. Search before posting — recommendations from members with visible results are the most useful.

**Google Maps.** "Afro hair salon Leeds" or "natural hair Leeds". Read reviews for mentions of natural and Afro-textured hair types specifically.

**Chapeltown and Harehills.** These areas have the highest concentration of Afro hair businesses in Leeds. Worth walking through if you want to assess a salon in person before booking.

## Outside Leeds City

Bradford, Wakefield, and Huddersfield each have some Afro hair provision, though more limited than Leeds. If you're in a satellite town with no local option, mobile stylists covering the West Yorkshire area are an alternative. Some Leeds stylists will travel.

Nottingham has a significant Afro-Caribbean and Black British community, particularly in the St Ann's, Hyson Green, and Radford areas, and an Afro hair salon offering that's broadly proportionate. Hyson Green in particular has historically had the highest concentration of Afro hair and Black-owned beauty businesses in the city.

**Nottingham water:** moderately hard to hard. Severn Trent serves most of Nottingham with moderately hard water. A chelating shampoo monthly to bi-monthly is worthwhile for natural hair in Nottingham.

## How to Find a Nottingham Afro Hair Salon

**Instagram.** Search "#NottinghamNaturalHair", "#NottinghamBraids", "#AfroHairNottingham". Instagram is the most reliable source for current, active stylists.

**Facebook groups.** Nottingham-based Black community and natural hair groups. Recommendations with visible results are the most useful signals.

**Google Maps.** "Afro hair salon Nottingham" or "natural hair Nottingham". Read reviews for natural hair mentions specifically.

**Hyson Green.** The highest density of Afro hair businesses in Nottingham, including Black-owned beauty supply stores where you can ask for recommendations.

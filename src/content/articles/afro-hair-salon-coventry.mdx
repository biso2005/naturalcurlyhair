Coventry has a substantial Afro-Caribbean and Black African community, particularly in Hillfields, Foleshill, and Wood End, and an Afro hair salon offering proportionate to this community. It's frequently overlooked in wider UK Afro hair guides that jump from Birmingham to Nottingham — but for people in Coventry and Warwickshire, it's the nearest major centre.

## How to Find a Coventry Afro Hair Salon

**Instagram.** Search "#CoventryNaturalHair", "#CoventryBraids", "#AfroHairCoventry". Also check "#WestMidlandsHair" for stylists who cover the Coventry–Birmingham corridor.

**Facebook groups.** Coventry Black community groups and Midlands natural hair groups. Coventry's community networks are closely connected to Birmingham's — recommendations often cross between the two cities.

**Google Maps.** "Afro hair salon Coventry" or "natural hair Coventry". Hillfields returns the most results.

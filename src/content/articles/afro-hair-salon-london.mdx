London has the most concentrated range of Afro hair specialists in the UK. It also has the widest variation in quality, the highest prices, and the most salons that list "Afro hair" without the experience to back it. Finding the right salon in London is less about scarcity and more about assessment.

**London water:** very hard to extremely hard throughout Greater London. Thames Water data consistently shows hardness above 300mg/L in most areas. Monthly chelating shampoo is non-negotiable for natural hair in London.

---

## How to Find a London Afro Hair Salon

**Instagram.** London has the most active natural hair stylist community on Instagram in the UK. Search area-specific tags — "#NorthLondonNaturalHair", "#SouthLondonBraids", "#EastLondonLocs" — plus the style you want. A stylist whose portfolio shows consistent, quality work on your hair type over multiple years is a reliable starting point.

**Google Maps.** Search "natural hair salon [area]" or "Afro hair [borough]". Read reviews for mentions of type 4 hair, natural styles, and locs specifically. Star rating alone tells you less than the content of the reviews.

**Treatwell and Fresha.** Both platforms have "Afro hair" as a service category with London coverage. Compare portfolios and prices; read reviews that specifically mention your hair type.

**Facebook groups.** "Natural Hair London", "Afro Hair London", and borough-specific natural hair groups are active. Search before posting — most common questions have been answered. Recommendations from members whose hair you can see are the most reliable.

**Naturally Tribal directory.** Naturally Tribal (naturallytriba.co.uk) maintains a UK directory of natural hair stylists with a significant London component.

## What to Look For

- Portfolio showing your specific hair type (not a mix of relaxed, natural, and other textures presented as equivalent experience)
- Prices disclosed before booking — London salons charging above the UK average should be able to justify what they offer
- Willingness to consult before a major service

## Areas With Concentration of Afro Hair Specialists

Peckham, Brixton, Tottenham, Hackney, Harlesden, Lewisham, and Woolwich historically have the highest concentration of Afro hair salons in London. This reflects community demographics and doesn't mean salons outside these areas are worse — but these are where the search yields the most options.

If you notice early in the appointment that the stylist doesn't have the experience they implied — ask questions about the technique they're about to use. **How much should I expect to pay for braids in London?**

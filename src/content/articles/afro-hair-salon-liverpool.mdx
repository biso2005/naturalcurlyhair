Liverpool has one of the oldest established Black communities in the UK, centred historically in Toxteth (Liverpool 8). This history has produced an Afro hair salon community that predates the natural hair movement — experienced stylists with decades of practice are more common here than in cities where the community is newer. The natural hair specialist offering has grown alongside the broader UK natural hair scene.

**Liverpool water:** mostly soft to moderately soft. United Utilities data shows Liverpool water on the softer end of the northwest range. Significantly softer than London or the Midlands. Natural hair in Liverpool experiences less mineral buildup than in harder-water cities.

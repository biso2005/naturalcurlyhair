As I Am is the most formulation-consistent natural hair brand available in the UK. While SheaMoisture's reputation has been complicated by Unilever acquisition changes and Cantu has some products that ride the brand name, the As I Am core range has stayed close to what made it popular. The range is smaller than SheaMoisture but more reliably good across the board.

Available primarily at Boots and Superdrug. Prices are mid-range for natural hair brands.

---

## Worth Buying

**As I Am Coconut CoWash Cleansing Conditioner** — the benchmark co-wash product in the natural hair community, and still the best option in UK retail for type 3 and 4 hair. Rinses cleanly, adds slip, and doesn't leave the hair limp. A proper co-wash, not just a conditioner repackaged. Available at Boots and Superdrug. Around £9 for 454g.

**As I Am Leave-In Conditioner** — water as the first ingredient, which is the right formulation for a leave-in. Provides enough conditioning and slip to detangle and serve as the base layer in a full routine. Works for type 3A through 4B. For very dense 4C, the Cantu Leave-In is heavier and may work better. Around £11 for 237ml.

**As I Am Curl Clarity Shampoo** — one of the better sulphate-free clarifying shampoos in UK retail. Genuinely removes buildup without being as stripping as an SLS shampoo. Appropriate for monthly clarifying as part of a sulphate-free routine. Around £10 for 237ml.

**As I Am Double Butter Rich Moisturizer** — the heaviest product in the range and appropriate for it. For very dry 4C hair or hair that's lost significant moisture, the butter content provides a genuine moisture seal. Not for everyday use on most hair types — it's too heavy — but as a treatment or for particularly dry hair in winter. Around £12 for 227g.

## Skip These

**As I Am Naturally Straight** — a heat protectant marketed for natural hair transitioning to heat styling. It's a fine heat protectant but not meaningfully differentiated from other heat protectants at lower price points. No reason to choose it over a generic heat protectant unless you're already committed to the As I Am range.

**As I Am Rice Water Collection** — the rice water marketing trend has produced a range that the formulations don't fully justify at the price. The rice water provides mild protein, but the products in this sub-range are priced above their performance relative to the core As I Am line. The original CoWash and Leave-In outperform the Rice Water styling products for most hair types.

## The Range's Strengths and Weaknesses

**Strengths:**
- Formulation consistency — the CoWash and Leave-In today perform the same as they did five years ago
- Water-first formulations across most of the range, which is the correct approach for hair care
- Silicone-free
- Generally moderate glycerin (lower than SheaMoisture, comparable to Cantu) — more consistent performance across UK seasons

**Weaknesses:**
- Smaller range than SheaMoisture — limited options for specific concerns (protein treatment, deep conditioning) compared to competitors
- Less widely distributed than Cantu — not in all supermarkets, primarily Boots and Superdrug
- The newer sub-ranges (Rice Water, Curl Control) are less strong than the original core products

## How the Core Three Work Together

The As I Am routine most people end up with:

1. **Curl Clarity Shampoo** monthly (clarifying)
2. **CoWash** between wash days or fortnightly
3. **Leave-In** after washing as the moisture base

This three-product core works for most type 3 and 4A–4B hair without needing products from any other brand. For 4C specifically, the Leave-In as the base and Cantu or a heavier curl cream on top fills the gap.

## FAQs

**Is As I Am good for 4C hair?**

The CoWash and Curl Clarity Shampoo work well for 4C. The Leave-In is appropriate for fine-to-medium 4C; for very dense or coarse 4C, a heavier leave-in (Cantu) works better. The Double Butter is the most 4C-appropriate styling product in the range.

**As I Am vs SheaMoisture vs Cantu — which brand should I start with?**

For beginners building a first natural hair routine: As I Am Coconut CoWash + As I Am Leave-In + Cantu Coconut Curling Cream is a solid, affordable starting trio widely available at Boots. Add SheaMoisture Manuka Honey Masque for deep conditioning. That combination covers the routine for most type 3 and 4 hair without spending heavily.

**Is As I Am still an independent brand?**

As I Am was founded by Dr. Willie Morrow and has changed ownership over time. As of this writing it's distributed under the Design Essentials parent company. Unlike SheaMoisture's transition to Unilever, the brand's formulations have remained more consistent through ownership changes.

**Does As I Am work for type 2 hair?**

The Leave-In and CoWash work for 2C hair. For 2A and 2B, the brand skews slightly heavy — the Coconut CoWash may weigh down finer wavy hair. A lighter brand like Umberto Giannini or Kinky Curly is better matched to looser curl types.

Sheffield has a smaller Afro hair salon scene than Leeds or Manchester, reflecting a proportionally smaller Afro-Caribbean community. The offering is real but concentrated — Burngreave, Firth Park, and Page Hall have historically had the most Afro hair businesses, and mobile stylists fill the gaps across the wider Sheffield area.

**Sheffield water:** mostly soft to moderately soft. Yorkshire Water serves Sheffield, and the Pennine catchment area delivers water that's softer than the Midlands or South East. Natural hair in Sheffield is less likely to experience significant mineral buildup than in harder-water cities, though a quarterly chelating wash is still good practice.

## How to Find a Sheffield Afro Hair Salon

**Instagram.** Search "#SheffieldNaturalHair", "#SheffieldBraids", "#AfroHairSheffield". The Sheffield natural hair community is smaller on Instagram than in larger cities but active; check also "#SouthYorkshireHair" for stylists covering the wider area.

**Facebook groups.** Sheffield-based Black community groups and natural hair groups. Some mobile stylists post directly in these groups.

**Google Maps.** "Afro hair salon Sheffield" or "natural hair Sheffield". The results set is smaller than in Leeds or Manchester; read reviews carefully for natural hair experience.

---

## Outside Sheffield

Rotherham, Doncaster, and Barnsley have very limited Afro hair salon provision. Mobile stylists covering South Yorkshire are the practical option for surrounding towns. Leeds is the nearest major city with a broader salon offering.

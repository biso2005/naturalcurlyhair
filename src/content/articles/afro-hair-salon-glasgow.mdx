Glasgow has a smaller Afro hair salon offering than comparable English cities, reflecting a proportionally smaller Black British community, though this has grown substantially over the past decade with increased African and Caribbean communities settling in the city. The natural hair scene in Glasgow is active, particularly online and through mobile stylists who cover the city and wider Central Belt.

**Glasgow water:** very soft. Scottish Water delivers some of the softest water in the UK — Loch Katrine-sourced water serving most of Glasgow has very low mineral content. Natural hair in Glasgow is at minimal risk of hard water mineral buildup. Regular chelating shampoo is not necessary; a basic clarifying shampoo is sufficient if you use heavy products.

## How to Find a Glasgow Afro Hair Salon

**Instagram.** Search "#GlasgowNaturalHair", "#GlasgowBraids", "#AfroHairGlasgow", "#ScottishNaturalHair". Some of the most active Scottish natural hair stylists serve the Glasgow and Edinburgh areas. Mobile stylists are well-represented.

**Facebook groups.** Glasgow Black community groups and Scottish natural hair groups (some cover both Edinburgh and Glasgow). Word of mouth in these communities is often more reliable than directory searches for a smaller salon scene.

**Google Maps.** "Afro hair salon Glasgow" or "natural hair Glasgow". The results set is smaller than in major English cities; read reviews carefully for natural hair experience specifically.

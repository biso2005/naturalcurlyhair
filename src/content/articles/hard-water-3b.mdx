Hard water leaves calcium and magnesium deposits on the hair
shaft. These deposits block moisture from entering — which
means your leave-in is not failing, the water is preventing
it from working.

This article covers what hard water does to 3B curls, how to
test whether it is your problem, and what actually fixes it.

Manchester has one of the UK's larger Afro hair salon communities outside London, concentrated primarily in South Manchester — Moss Side, Hulme, Rusholme, and Fallowfield — and in Salford. The natural hair salon offering has grown significantly over the past five years, though specialist natural hair salons (as distinct from salons that also offer Afro services) remain fewer than in London.

**Manchester water:** mostly soft to moderately soft. United Utilities data shows Manchester water significantly softer than London or the East of England. Natural hair in Manchester is less vulnerable to mineral buildup than in harder-water cities, though a quarterly chelating shampoo is still good practice.

---

## How to Find a Manchester Afro Hair Salon

**Instagram.** Search "#ManchesterNaturalHair", "#ManchesterBraids", "#AfroHairManchester" and the specific style you want. Manchester has an active natural hair stylist community on Instagram; area tags surface stylists across Greater Manchester including Salford, Trafford, and Stockport.

**Facebook groups.** "Black hair Manchester", "Natural hair Manchester", and Moss Side or Hulme community groups are the most active routes to recommendations. Recommendations from members whose hair is visible are the most reliable signal.

**Google Maps.** Search "Afro hair salon Manchester" or "natural hair salon Manchester". Read reviews for specific mentions of natural hair and type 4 hair rather than general ratings.

**Treatwell and Fresha.** Both platforms list Manchester salons with Afro hair service categories. Compare service menus — a salon that lists braids, locs, and natural styling is a different credential from one that lists "all hair types."

---

## Areas to Search

Moss Side, Hulme, and Rusholme have historically had the highest concentration of Afro hair salons in Manchester. Stretford, Trafford, and Salford also have options. The city centre has fewer specialist options but mobile stylists cover the broader Greater Manchester area.

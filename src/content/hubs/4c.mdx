4C coils have the tightest, most densely packed pattern on the spectrum. The zig-zag is very fine, shrinkage is extreme, and the hair requires consistent deep moisture to prevent breakage. Many products that work on 3C or 4A will be too light.

This hub covers every tested routine, product verdict, and technique specific to 4C coils.

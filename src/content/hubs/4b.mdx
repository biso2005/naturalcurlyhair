4B coils differ from 4A in their shape: instead of a spiral, the hair bends in sharp angles — a Z or zig-zag pattern rather than a coil. This makes definition harder to achieve and moisture harder to retain.

This hub covers every tested routine, product verdict, and technique specific to 4B coils in UK conditions.

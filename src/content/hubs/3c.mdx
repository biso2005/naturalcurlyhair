3C curls form a tight corkscrew spiral, denser and more compact than 3B. The pattern packs significant volume and is prone to dryness because natural oils struggle to travel down the tightly wound shaft.

This hub covers every tested routine, product verdict, and technique specific to 3C curls in UK conditions.

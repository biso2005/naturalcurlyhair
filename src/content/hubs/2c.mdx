2C waves have the most definition in the wave category. The S-pattern is clear and consistent, with a tendency toward frizz that increases with humidity. Products that work for 2A will often be too light; those for 3A may be too heavy.

This hub covers every tested routine, product verdict, and technique specific to 2C waves.

3A curls form a large, open spiral roughly the circumference of a thick marker. The pattern is clearly a curl rather than a wave, but it sits at the looser end of the curl spectrum and is easily disrupted by heavy products or rough handling.

This hub covers every tested routine, product verdict, and technique specific to 3A curls.

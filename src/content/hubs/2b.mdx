2B waves form a more pronounced S-shape than 2A, with more volume at the roots and a tendency to frizz in humidity. The pattern holds definition better than 2A but still needs lightweight products.

This hub covers every tested routine, product verdict, and technique specific to 2B waves in UK conditions.

3B curls are defined by a springy spiral roughly the circumference of a marker pen. The pattern holds definition well when moisture is balanced — but tips toward frizz when products are too heavy or too light.

This hub covers every tested routine, product verdict, and technique specific to 3B hair in UK conditions.

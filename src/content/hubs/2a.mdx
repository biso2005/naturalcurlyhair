2A waves sit closest to straight hair on the curl spectrum. The pattern is subtle — a soft, open S-shape that can be encouraged with the right products or lost entirely with the wrong ones.

This hub covers every tested routine, product verdict, and technique specific to 2A waves in UK conditions.

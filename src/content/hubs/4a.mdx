4A coils form a small, defined S-coil pattern. The circumference is tight and the density is high, which means significant shrinkage and a greater need for moisture than looser curl types. The pattern is clearly defined when hydrated.

This hub covers every tested routine, product verdict, and technique specific to 4A coils in UK conditions.
